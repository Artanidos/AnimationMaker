How to get a FREE license

In order to get a free license you just have to tell your friends or colleagues about AnimationMaker. If they buy a license from us you will also get a license in return.
Examples:

  • One of your colleagues is buying a 6 months license and you will get a free 2 months license.
  • One of your colleagues is buying a 12 months license and you will get a free 4 months license.
  • Three of your colleagues are buying a 12 months license and you will also get a free 12 months license.

It's very easy.
Just enter your email to the form on the right, click the "Create Link" button and copy the "link" and send it to a friend or colleague via email or post the link on social media. If someone clicks the link he or she will be leaded to our website and we will remember them. If they will come back later and buy a license we will remember that you sent them to our website and then we will send you the license via email.
